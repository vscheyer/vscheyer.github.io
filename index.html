FormFit

Running detection and correction.

FormFit is a running form detector and corrector aimed at helping long distance runners prevent shin splints. It works by targeting three common causes of pain: pelvic tilt, stride frequency, and heel striking. Using analysis of accelerometers mounted on the foot and back, FormFit lets you know when you exhibit potentially harmful running form.
